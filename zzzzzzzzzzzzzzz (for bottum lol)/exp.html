The Vijayanagara Empire was the last Hindu kingdom of the era,

Which was subsequently annexed by the Muslim sultantes, Nizam of Hydrabad and The British after its decline.

It was founded by Harihara Raya and Bukka Raya in 1336 AD

They were originaly serving under the Kakatiya Rulers of of Warangal

after the dicline of the Kakatiya rulers, they proclamed their independence and founded a new city on the southern bank of the river Tungabhadra

It was called The Vijayanagara meaning the city of victory

With the decline of Hoysala Kingdom,

Harihara Raya and Bukka Raya Expanded their kingdom and by 1346 AD,

They brought the enirity of Hoysala kingdom under their control.
