Divisibility rule for 8

If you divide a number by 8 and you get a whole number as an answer

(quotient), then that number is divisible by 8.


Instead of simply dividing your number by 8 to see if you get a whole

number, you can use what we call the Divisibility Rule for 8.

The Divisibility Rule for 8 is used to determine if a number is divisible by

8. You can also call it the test of divisibility for 8.
